A little about us.

Hi! We're the Union County Early College STEM Club. We're a group of students passionate about all things science, technology, engineering, and math. Since 2015, we've been working on a variety of projects to help us learn more.

One of our main focuses is robotics. Our teams compete in the biannual UCPS Robotics Competition using the year's VEX Robotics Challenge. We compete alongside the teams from other schools in a main bracket and in other timed and automated challenges.

We also participate in a variety of other STEM projects including underwater ROVs, turning fruits into instruments, and Rube Goldberg machines.
