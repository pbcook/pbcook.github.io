Project Overview

The STEM Club at Union County Early College High School (UCEC) would like a website to showcase some of the projects the club is working on, as well as provide various relevant other information relating to the club.

The main users of this website will be other students and staff within the school who would like to view information relating to the STEM club. Students would also be able to use the site to sign up for the club if interested.

The contents of the site will include a gallery showcasing the past and current projects the STEM club is working on. In addition, there will be separate pages including a page about the club, upcoming meeting times and events, and a sign-up form.
