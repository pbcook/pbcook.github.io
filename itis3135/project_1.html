Project 1

Project Overview

The STEM Club at Union County Early College High School (UCEC) would like a website to showcase some of the projects the club is working on, as well as provide various relevant other information relating to the club.

The main users of this website will be other students and staff within the school who would like to view information relating to the STEM club. Students would also be able to use the site to sign up for the club if interested.

The contents of the site will include a gallery showcasing the past and current projects the STEM club is working on. In addition, there will be separate pages including a page about the club, upcoming meeting times and events, and a sign-up form.

Page Design

  • Home: The home page acts as the first seen webpage, providing a brief introduction, an overview of the content to the website, and relevant important links. This page will be used by all viewers of the webpage. There will not be data entry on the page. There will be hyperlinks, primarily to other parts of the website.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Home: This page will be used as a landing page for all users of the site, displaying a main image and welcome message and a small blurb about the club, with relevant links to other pages as needed. There won't be any data entry on the page. There will likely be hyperlinks to other pages included in the text, as well as the image being a link that takes you to the main gallery.
  • About: The about page provides more in-depth information about the club. This page will be used by all viewers who are interested in learning more about the STEM Club. There will not be data entry on the page, and there are no planned links as of now.
  • Events: The events page will provide relevant information about upcoming meeting times as well as competitions and other events relevant to the STEM Club. This page will be used by all users, particularly those interested in joining the STEM Club or attending an event. There will not be data entry on the page, but there will be hyperlinks to external pages about some of the events. There also could be an imbedded map showing the location of some events.
  • Gallery: The gallery will provide a variety of images to showcase the projects the STEM Club is working on, and provide brief context for each image. The page will be used by all users who want to know more about the projects the STEM Club is working on. There won't be data entry, but there may be links to pages going more in-depth about some projects. There will be buttons for navigating through the images in the slideshow.
  • Sign Up: The sign up page will provide the ability to enter information to sign up for the STEM Club. The page will be used by students who would like to join the club. There will be data entry for the form on the page, and some fields will require validations. There may be relevant hyperlinks for contact information. There will be a button that will submit the data when clicked.
